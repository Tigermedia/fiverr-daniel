# Hosting Recommendations for Air Titan AC Repair

## Quick Comparison for Your Local SEO Project

### Option 1: Cloudways (Recommended for Value)
- **Cost**: $12/month (DigitalOcean server)
- **Performance**: Excellent (cloud-based, fast)
- **Management**: Fully managed (no technical maintenance)
- **Scaling**: Easy to upgrade as business grows
- **Support**: 24/7 expert support
- **Best for**: Performance + value combination

### Option 2: SiteGround (Recommended for Beginners)  
- **Cost**: $15/month (GrowBig plan)
- **Performance**: Very good (optimized for WordPress)
- **Management**: Fully managed with cPanel
- **WordPress Focus**: Specialized WordPress features
- **Support**: Excellent WordPress-specific help
- **Best for**: WordPress beginners, local businesses

### Option 3: WP Engine (Premium Option)
- **Cost**: $25/month (Startup plan)
- **Performance**: Excellent (enterprise-grade)
- **Management**: Premium managed WordPress
- **Developer Tools**: Advanced staging, Git integration
- **Support**: Premium WordPress expertise
- **Best for**: High-traffic sites, advanced users

## For Your HVAC Business Specifically

**Cloudways** offers the best value - better performance than WordPress.com at half the cost of your current Premium plan, with room to scale as your local SEO automation drives more traffic.

**All options include:**
- US-based servers (better for Louisiana customers)
- Free SSL certificates
- Daily automated backups  
- CDN for faster loading
- Full control for our automation optimizations

**Bottom line**: Any of these will give you 95-100% automation effectiveness vs. ~75% on WordPress.com Premium.
